Our Facilities

The Faculty of Philology offers a modern and supportive academic environment equipped to meet the highest standards of teaching, research, and student development. Facilities include spacious lecture halls, multimedia classrooms, language laboratories, and a well-resourced academic library.
Students benefit from access to specialized translation and interpretation laboratories, computer labs, and dedicated research and study centers, designed to enhance practical skills and independent learning.
To support campus life, the Faculty also features a comfortable student café, an on-site bookshop, and a Student Support Center offering academic guidance, mental health services, and career counseling. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the campus, along with quiet study spaces and a calendar of academic and cultural events, creating a dynamic and inclusive atmosphere for all.

World Class Library

The Faculty’s library is a cornerstone of academic life, offering a vast and carefully curated collection of books, journals, and digital resources in the fields of language, literature, linguistics, and cultural studies. Equipped with modern facilities, reading rooms, and quiet study areas, the library supports both individual research and collaborative learning. With access to international databases and expert library staff, students and researchers benefit from a truly world-class academic resource.

Largest Play Ground

The Faculty is home to a spacious and modern indoor playground, designed to encourage physical activity and enhance student life throughout the year. Equipped for various sports and recreational activities—including basketball, volleyball, indoor fitness, and group exercise—the facility provides a safe and climate-controlled environment. It also serves as a venue for student events, wellness programs, and interfaculty tournaments, promoting health, teamwork, and community engagement within an academic setting.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The Faculty cafeteria offers a comfortable and welcoming space where students and staff can enjoy a variety of freshly prepared meals, snacks, and beverages. With affordable prices, a diverse menu, and a relaxed atmosphere, it serves as a popular gathering place for breaks between classes, informal meetings, and social interaction. The cafeteria is committed to quality, hygiene, and friendly service.
